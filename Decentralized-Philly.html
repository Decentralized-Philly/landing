Blockchain and Web3 Events in Philly

Decentralized Philly is a community of people who come together because they care about growing Philadelphia’s Blockchain and Web3 ecosystem.

Building a Decentralized Future
Our members include tech-savvy developers, ambitious students, forward-thinking entrepreneurs, and seasoned professionals united by a passion for exploring the potential of Web3 and blockchain.

Our Mission

  • Make Philadelphia the industry leading city for innovation in blockchain and web3 technology
  • Provide educational opportunities to help individuals advance their career in this industry
  • Create an environment that facilitates personal and professional relationships and growth

Our Vision

  • Decentralized Philly is driven by a strong belief in the transformative potential of blockchain and Web3 technology. We envision a future where power and control are distributed more equitably, and we actively explore how blockchain and Web3 technology can reshape various industries. We see a future where Philadelphia uses this new technology to address pressing social issues and promote a more inclusive, transparent, and sustainable future.

What we do

  • Community Events: Decentralized Philly uses Meetup.com, Eventbrite, and Twitter to organize events where we focus on exploring the intersection of blockchain technology and its practical applications in various industries. The purpose of these events is to provide a welcoming and engaging environment where members can discuss, learn, and collaborate on blockchain-related topics. 

  • Collaborations: Decentralized Philly is a community that values collaboration and collective problem-solving. We encourage partnerships among members to co-create projects, explore use cases, and tackle real-world challenges using decentralized technologies. Our events provide a social environment for networking, knowledge sharing, and innovation to flourish.
